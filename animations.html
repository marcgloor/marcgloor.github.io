blender animations and images
by Marc O. Gloor

Linux-Inside Logo modelled in blender (using rotoscoping from an intel inside font image) on a Sun Sparc running linux. Sequence length is about 80 images, they were rendered on a 6 node mosix cluster. Compiled to endless loop animated gifs using mogrify and convert which are part of the imagemagick package. The base model can be downloaded here. If you need another color combination for your website, send me an email. Published unter the OpenContent License. The linuxine image was also modelled and rendered in blender.
