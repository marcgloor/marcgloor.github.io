XFCE logo animations
by Marc Gloor

A collection of rendered xfce animations for public use

For the xfce community, I produced various logo animations for their websites, just use these images with a link to xfce.org. Logo modelled in blender (using rotoscoping from an xfce font image) on a Sun Sparc running Linux. Sequence length is about 80 images, they were rendered on a 6 node mosix loadbalancing cluster. Compiled to endless loop animated gifs using mogrify and convert, part of imagemagick. The hardest work (base model) can be downloaded here. Published unter the OpenContent License.
