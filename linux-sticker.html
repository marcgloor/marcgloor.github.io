Note about the missing copyrights and trademarks on the sticker

After publishing the sticker on a web newsboard in early 2003 (not in usenet) an anonymous protectionist flamed me first about the loveless and bad design from my stickers and secondly that I waived to include the copyright © and (tm) of OpenGroup Unix and Torvalds' Linux trademarks. In addition to our short webboard debate, he flamed also against the fanciless Samba project and the Bazaar (citation: Tauschbörsenfreaks) of the OpenSource community. I answered that the sticker was intended for Linux users who likes to show their friends that they run a fast Unix derivate on their box. My statement was that the sticker was primary designed for users which like their operating system considering Unix as a high performance operating system. Thanks to the anonymous contributor with his flame for helping me optimize my sticker offer.
